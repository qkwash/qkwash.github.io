We follow strict covid-19 protocols

we are continuously monitoring the latest recommendations from leading health organizations such as WHO and CDC . Best practices are being constantly communicated to ensure that customers and employees know how to protect themselves and others during appointments. As part of social distancing, we suggest a big wave and a simple "hello" instead of a handshake when greeting us. For a contactless appointment, feel free to message us before we arrive to share instructions about how we can access your vehicle without having to come outside to meet them. You can specify your car concerns, authorize repairs and pay without any contact. Additionally, the best method of germ spread is prevention, so please wash your hands frequently, and keep an appropriate 6-feet distance away from us.
